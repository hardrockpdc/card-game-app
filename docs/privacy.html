Card Night — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

What we collect

Card Night does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server.

What's stored on your device

All gameplay data — your profile name, photo, coin balance, game statistics, and saved games — is stored only on your device using your device's local storage. None of this data leaves your device.

Camera and photo library

If you choose to set a profile photo, Card Night uses your camera or photo library only at the moment you tap that option. The photo is stored only on your device.

Local network

Card Night uses your local Wi-Fi network only for direct device-to-device multiplayer. No third-party servers are involved. No data is logged or shared.

Third parties

Card Night contains no third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs.

Children

Card Night does not collect data from any user, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/hardrockpdc/card-game-app/issues.
